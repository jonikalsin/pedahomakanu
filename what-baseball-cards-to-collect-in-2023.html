What Baseball Cards to Collect in 2023

As we approach 2023, the baseball card collecting hobby is more popular than ever. With a plethora of options and cards to choose from, collectors are always on the lookout for the next big thing. In this comprehensive guide, we will discuss the top baseball cards to collect in 2023, covering everything from vintage classics to modern rookies, and even signed sports memorabilia from Fan Arch. By the end of this article, you'll have a solid understanding of the baseball cards worth adding to your collection in the coming year.

1. Vintage Classics

1.1 Babe Ruth Cards

No baseball card collection is complete without at least one Babe Ruth card. Despite the passage of time, the iconic slugger's cards continue to be in high demand. In 2023, consider adding a 1933 Goudey Babe Ruth (#144, #149, #181, or #53) or a 1916 M101-4/5 Sporting News Babe Ruth to your collection. These cards are not only valuable but also hold significant historical importance.

1.2 Mickey Mantle Cards

Another classic player to consider for your collection is Mickey Mantle. His 1952 Topps card is one of the most sought-after baseball cards of all time. Other notable Mantle cards include the 1951 Bowman Mickey Mantle Rookie Card and the 1969 Topps Mickey Mantle. While these cards may be expensive, their value will likely continue to grow over time.

1.3 Honus Wagner T206

The T206 Honus Wagner card is one of the rarest and most valuable baseball cards of all time. With only around 60 known to exist, the T206 Wagner is a true collector's item. If you're lucky enough to come across one, it's worth considering as a long-term investment.

2.1 Wander Franco

Wander Franco, a young player with immense potential, is a hot prospect for collectors. His 2019 Bowman Chrome Prospect Autograph card is one to watch in 2023. With a promising career ahead of him, Franco's cards are likely to appreciate in value over time.

2.2 Vladimir Guerrero Jr.

Another exciting player to keep an eye on is Vladimir Guerrero Jr. His 2016 Bowman Chrome Prospect Autograph card is a popular choice for collectors. As Guerrero Jr. continues to develop as a player, his cards are sure to rise in value.

2.3 Shohei Ohtani

Shohei Ohtani is a unique talent, excelling both as a pitcher and a hitter. His 2018 Bowman Chrome Rookie Autograph card is a must-have for any serious collector. With Ohtani's incredible skillset, his cards are poised for significant appreciation in the coming years.

3. Hall of Famers

3.1 Derek Jeter

Derek Jeter, the New York Yankees legend, is a popular choice for baseball card collectors. His 1993 SP Foil Rookie Card (#279) and 1993 Topps Rookie Card (#98) are both great options to consider adding to your collection in 2023.

3.2 Ken Griffey Jr.

Ken Griffey Jr. is another Hall of Famer with a strong following among collectors. His 1989 Upper Deck Rookie Card (#1) and 1989 Fleer Glossy Rookie Card (#548) are both iconic cards that would make excellent additions to any collection.

3.3 Cal Ripken Jr.

Cal Ripken Jr., known for his incredible longevity and ironman streak, is a popular choice for collectors. His 1982 Topps Traded Rookie Card (#98T) and 1982 Donruss Rookie Card (#405) are both excellent options for those looking to add a Hall of Famer to their collection in 2023.

4. Top Prospects

4.1 Adley Rutschman

Adley Rutschman, the first overall pick in the 2019 MLB Draft, is a top prospect to watch. His 2019 Bowman Draft Chrome Autograph card is an excellent choice for collectors looking to invest in potential future stars.

4.2 Julio Rodriguez

Another exciting prospect, Julio Rodriguez, is making waves in the baseball world. His 2019 Bowman Chrome Prospect Autograph card is a popular choice for collectors looking to get in early on a potential superstar.

4.3 Bobby Witt Jr.

Bobby Witt Jr., the second overall pick in the 2019 MLB Draft, is another top prospect worth considering. His 2020 Bowman Chrome Draft Autograph card is one to watch in 2023, as his career continues to develop.

5.1 Topps Chrome Refractors

Topps Chrome Refractors are popular among collectors due to their limited print runs and eye-catching designs. Consider adding a Refractor of a star player, such as Mike Trout or Ronald Acu√±a Jr., to your collection in 2023.

5.2 Panini Prizm Color Parallels

Panini Prizm Color Parallels offer a unique twist on standard cards, with colorful backgrounds that make them stand out. Keep an eye out for Color Parallels of top players and prospects in 2023.

5.3 Bowman Sterling Continuity Autographs

Bowman Sterling Continuity Autographs are limited-edition cards featuring on-card autographs from top prospects and stars. With a sleek design and a focus on young talent, these cards are a great addition to any collection in 2023.

6. Autographed Cards

6.1 Topps Five Star Autographs

Topps Five Star Autographs are high-end cards featuring on-card autographs from some of baseball's biggest stars. Look for autographed cards from players such as Mike Trout, Aaron Judge, and Juan Soto in 2023.

6.2 Bowman Chrome Prospect Autographs

Bowman Chrome Prospect Autographs are a popular choice for collectors looking to invest in young talent. Keep an eye out for autographed cards from top prospects like Adley Rutschman, Julio Rodriguez, and Bobby Witt Jr. in 2023.

6.3 Signed Sports Memorabilia from Fan Arch

In addition to baseball cards, consider adding signed sports memorabilia to your collection. Fan Arch offers a wide range of authentic signed memorabilia from top athletes, including baseball stars. Autographed baseballs, jerseys, and photos can be a unique and valuable addition to your collection.

7. Error and Variation Cards

7.1 1990 Topps Frank Thomas No Name on Front

Error cards, such as the 1990 Topps Frank Thomas No Name on Front, are sought after by collectors due to their rarity. Keep an eye out for error cards and variations in 2023, as they can be valuable additions to your collection.

7.2 1989 Fleer Bill Ripken F-Face

Another famous error card is the 1989 Fleer Bill Ripken F-Face, which features an obscenity on the bat knob. This card is a popular choice for collectors, and adding one to your collection in 2023 could be a wise investment.

7.3 Modern Card Variations

Modern card manufacturers often release variations of base cards, featuring different images or backgrounds. Keep an eye out for these variations in 2023, as they can be a fun and potentially valuable addition to your collection.

8. Complete Sets

8.1 Topps Heritage

Topps Heritage sets are popular among collectors due to their retro designs and focus on current players. Consider adding a complete Topps Heritage set to your collection in 2023, as it offers a comprehensive look at the year's top players.

8.2 Bowman Draft

Bowman Draft sets are a great way to invest in the game's future stars. By collecting a complete Bowman Draft set, you'll have cards for a wide range of prospects, some of whom may become the next big stars in MLB.

8.3 Topps Allen & Ginter

Topps Allen & Ginter sets are known for their unique designs and eclectic mix of subjects, including baseball players, celebrities, and historical figures. Adding a complete Allen & Ginter set to your collection in 2023 can provide a diverse and interesting addition.

9. Graded Cards

9.1 PSA Graded Cards

Professional Sports Authenticator (PSA) graded cards are a popular choice for collectors, as they provide a guarantee of authenticity and condition. In 2023, consider adding high-grade PSA cards of star players and prospects to your collection.

9.2 Beckett Grading Services (BGS) Graded Cards

Beckett Grading Services (BGS) is another reputable grading company that collectors trust. Look for BGS-graded cards of top rookies, prospects, and Hall of Famers to add to your collection in 2023.

9.3 SGC Graded Cards

Sportscard Guaranty (SGC) is a third grading option for collectors. While not as well-known as PSA or BGS, SGC-graded cards can still be a valuable addition to your collection, especially if they feature high grades and desirable players.

10. Oddball and Unusual Cards

10.1 1963 Fleer Baseball Set

The 1963 Fleer Baseball set is an unusual and short-lived set that features only 66 cards. With its unique design and limited checklist, adding a complete 1963 Fleer set to your collection in 2023 could be an interesting choice.

10.2 Topps Project 2020

Topps Project 2020 is a unique set featuring reimagined versions of iconic baseball cards by various artists. With a limited print run and a diverse range of styles, these cards can be a fun and distinctive addition to your collection in 2023.

10.3 Food and Beverage Issued Cards

Cards issued by food and beverage companies, such as Kellogg's, Hostess, and Coca-Cola, can be an unusual and nostalgic addition to your collection. Look for these cards in 2023 to add a touch of nostalgia to your baseball card collection.

In conclusion, there are numerous baseball cards and memorabilia worth considering for your collection in 2023. From vintage classics to modern rookies, Hall of Famers to top prospects, and even signed sports memorabilia from Fan Arch, there's something for every collector. By keeping an eye on the trends and players mentioned in this guide, you'll be well-prepared to make smart additions to your collection in the coming year.
