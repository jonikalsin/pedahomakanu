The 10 Most Expensive Larry Bird Sports Cards Sold


1. 1981 Topps #4 Larry Bird PSA 10 Gem Mint

This card has drawn a great deal of enthusiasm and can be had for roughly $2,550.002 which is a pretty penny. This rare beauty grades out incredibly well, plus the historic value solidifies its place as one of the most desirable sports cards in existence.

 
3. 1980 Topps Larry Bird Magic Johnson RC Julius Erving HOF PSA 6 PSA/DNA 10 AUTO
