Malcolm Goodwin is a famous American actor who is best known for his role as Clive Babineaux in iZombie series on The CW, also as Oscar Finlay in Reacher series on Amazon Prime Video.

Career

Malcolm passion for acting was reaching the ceiling during his play in the Julia Richman Talent Unlimited Program in New York City. He polished his craft at the SUNY Purchase College Acting Conservatory which earned him a bachelor’s degree in Theatre Arts and Film.

Goodwin has been directing, producing, and creating independent commercials, sketches, short films, music videos, and public service announcements.

In 2011, he made an appearance in LMFAO’s music video for the hit song Party Rock Anthem. His talent and influence have been acknowledged in articles featured in publications like Interview, L.A. Confidential, King, Vibe, and Venice.

He has taken on minor roles in various films and TV shows that includes American Gangster, Detroit 1-8-7, Leatherheads, The Longshots, and Crazy on the Outside.
