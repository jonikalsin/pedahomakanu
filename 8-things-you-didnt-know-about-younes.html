8 Things You Didnt Know About Younes Bendjima

➡ Younes Bendjima Biography

Younes Bendjima is an Algerian model, who has worked for various brands including Ralph Lauren and Calvin Klein. Also, he has established himself as the best model. He is a former boxer who turned model and rose to fame once he started dating Kim Kardashian in the year 2017, later they parted their ways in the year 2018. Well, there are more things to know about him as his fans wanted to know more about him.

8 Things You Didn’t Know About Younes Bendjima

Before fame

Before fame, Younes Bendjima initially started working in his father’s restaurant as there was no way out for him before joining the field of modeling. Later, he was approached by a scout to be a model in 2011.

He worked with various renowned brands

Younes Bendjima is undoubtedly a big name in the field of modeling and has worked with various popular brands, such as; Ralph Lauren and Calvin Klein.

His love, dating relationships

Younes Bendjima previously dating with his fellow model, Jourdan Dunn. But, the couple parted their ways when things didn’t well in their relationship.

Younes Bendjima & Kim Kardashian

The most celebrated stars dating each other in 2017, and later they parted their ways in the year 2018. After parting the ways, he signed with Next Model Management.

He speaks multiple languages

Younes does not just have a pretty face, but also fluent in many languages. He was born in Algeria and visited many countries. Younes is fluent in three languages, such as; Arabic, English, and French.

Younes Bendjima was a boxer

Before joining a career as a model, he was a boxer and has won many titles as well. Younes started his career as a boxer at the age of 11, got inspiration from Muhammad Ali and Mike Tyson, after reading about them. He got boxing training from his uncle Faycal, still, he trains three times a week.

His hobbies

Among his other interests and hobbies, he loves traveling and also seeks inspiration from different cultures as well.

He is a fitness freak

Younes cares about his health and toned physique and loves working out in a gym, exercise to keep in shape. Also, he loves to take care of his proper nutrition and diet to make himself fit and healthy.
