What happened to Brandon spikes?

Brandon Spikes is a former professional American football player who played as a linebacker in the National Football League (NFL). He was born on September 3, 1987, in Shelby, North Carolina. NFL Career

Suspension and Release

In 2015, Brandon Spikes was suspended by the NFL for violating the league's substance abuse policy. He was initially suspended for four games but later had his suspension increased to a full season.
