Christina Ricci Net Worth, Age, Boyfriend, Family, Biography &amp; More

Christina Ricci is an American who is a popular American actress by profession. She started her career in the year 1990.

Mermaids (1990) was her cinematic debut, followed by her breakthrough performance as Wednesday Addams in The Addams Family (1991) and its sequel.

Christina Ricci Wiki/Biography

Born on 12 February 1980, Christina Ricci’s age is 42 years as of 2022. She was born and raised in a well-settled Christian family from Santa Monica, California, United States. She is an American by nationality and has a belief in the Christian religion.

She completed her early schooling at Edgemont Elementary School and later got admission to a few other schools which are Glenfield Middle School, Montclair High School, and Morristown–Beard School. She lastly attended Professional Children’s School in New York City, United States. From the very beginning of her childhood, she was more interested in singing rather than in studying.
