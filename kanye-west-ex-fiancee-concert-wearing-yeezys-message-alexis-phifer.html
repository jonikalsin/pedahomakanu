Kanye West Ex Fiancee At Concert Wearing Yeezys Sending Him A Message? Hollywood Life

Kanye West, 39, had a very unexpected guest show up for his Saint Pablo concert on Oct. 26. The rap mogul’s ex-fiancee, Alexis Phifer, decided to go under the radar by attending his Los Angeles gig, that is until she took to social media with a pic of herself in skin-tight leather pants and a pair of Yeezys. Fans quickly speculated that Kanye gave her the pricey sneakers as a gift, wondering if she was starting drama or showing support. The former flames did have a lot of history, since they allegedly dated on-and-off for six years, first becoming exclusive in 2002.

The “famous” lyricist proposed to Alexis during a romantic vacation to Capri in 2006, reportedly popping the question while they dined on lobster and pasta. But, things fizzled out over time, leading Ye and Alexis to officially split in April 2008. The pair both had a passion for fashion, traveling the world together and venturing to many runway shows before parting ways. Alexis opened up about their cordial breakup at the time, revealing, “It’s always sad when things like this end, and we remain friends,” telling People. “I wish him the best in his future and all of his endeavors. He’s one of the most talented people I’ve ever met.”

It’s obvious Kanye is off the market, since he’s extremely devoted to his queen, Kim Kardashian. According to witnessing fans, the rapper paused his whole set to say a couple of words about his leading lady on the very same night Alexis was in attendance. “I’m so lucky to have my wife here right now,” he said while pointing at Kim in the audience. He also shared a heartwarming, tribute video he sent to the reality star in celebration of her 36th birthday.
