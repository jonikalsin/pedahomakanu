Dan + Shay Release Three New Tracks Ahead of the Release of Their Forthcoming Project, Bigger Hou

A new era of Dan + Shay begins TODAY (July 14th)!

As a part of this week’s New Music Friday, Dan Smyers and Shay Mooney hit fans with a thrilling triple release, as well as the announcement of their forthcoming album, Bigger Houses.

After revealing to their one million Instagram followers earlier this week that “Save Me The Trouble” would be their next single, Dan + Shay sweetened the deal with a VERY exciting announcement, ultimately sending all country music-lovers into a frenzy.

“Our new album, BIGGER HOUSES, will be available everywhere September 15th and pre-order begins tonight at 11 PM CT,” the legendary duo wrote in an Instagram caption. “In addition to SAVE ME THE TROUBLE, you’ll also get 2 more of our favorite songs, HEARTBREAK ON THE MAP, and the title track, BIGGER HOUSES. We poured every bit of our hearts into this album and truly cannot wait for y’all to hear it. New era starts now! 🏠”

If their new project is anything like “Save Me The Trouble,” “Heartbreak On The Map” and “Bigger Houses,” we know that it will be absolutely spectacular!

Aside from Dan + Shay’s extremely exciting release (and announcement), some more of today’s biggest country stars released music that we already have on repeat, including Brothers Osborne, Alana Springsteen, Colter Wall, Morgan Wade, Josh Ross, Hailey Whitters, Warren Zeiders, Colbie Caillat, Caylee Hammack, Wyatt Flores and more.

Claim To Fame star Logan Crosby even dropped his debut EP as a part of this week’s New Music Friday, featuring his most recent single, “Run Away With You.” The emerging artist told Celeb Secrets ALL about 23 Days In LA at CMA Fest last month — Click HERE to see the interview for yourself!

As if this week’s New Music Friday playlist could not get any better, there were several collaborations released this week that are sure to be climbing the charts in no time. Mickey Guyton and Kane Brown teamed up for “Nothing Compares To You,” Spencer Crandall and Cooper Alan teamed up for “Didn’t Do,” Caroline Jones and Zac Brown Band teamed up for “Million Little Bandaids” and more.

After listening to ALL of this week’s phenomenal releases, we managed to pinpoint some of the ones we will definitely be saving to our music library.

Keep scrolling to see which ones we picked, and be sure to let us know which track you like the best by either leaving a reaction at the bottom of the post, or by sending us a tweet at @CS_Country.
