Pooja Hegde’s Professional Career

Similarly, her second film was a Telugu film called Oka Laila Kosam, in which she co-starred with Naga Chaitanya. She was nominated for Best Actress at the 62nd Filmfare Awards for this (South). She made an appearance in the Telugu film Mukunda in 2014.

Later that year, she co-starred with Allu Arjun in the Telugu film Ala Vaikunthapurramuloo, which became one of the highest-grossing Telugu films, grossing over INR 250 crore.
